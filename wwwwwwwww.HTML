Frequently Asked Questions

How does the replica creation process work? ‚ñº
We use AI to learn your loved one's speech patterns, personality, and memories. The process requires a 1-minute voice sample, at least 3 photos, and personality info. Our team works with you over 4-6 weeks to refine the replica.
Is my data private and secure? ‚ñº
Absolutely. We encrypt all data, host on private infrastructure, and never share your info. You retain full ownership and control of your replica and its data.
Do I need family consent for this? ‚ñº
Yes. For post-mortem replicas, we require approval from all immediate family members. We believe in collaborative, respectful remembrance.
Can I update or delete the replica later? ‚ñº
You have full control. You can request updates based on new data or delete the replica entirely at any time.
Is this a healthy way to grieve? ‚ñº
We work with licensed grief counselors to ensure it's used responsibly as a supportive tool‚Äînever a substitute for real human connection and therapy.
